MonieTrack PRO Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 19, 2026

MonieTrack PRO (“MonieTrack,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

Important: MonieTrack is a personal spending and planning companion. We do not hold, transfer, lend, invest, or manage customer funds.

Information We Collect

  • Name and email address
  • Transactions entered by users
  • Savings goals and budgets
  • Bill reminders
  • Invoice information entered by users
  • Subscription status
  • Device and app usage information used to improve performance

How We Use Information

  • Provide access to MonieTrack PRO services
  • Synchronize data across devices
  • Process subscriptions through Paystack
  • Improve app features and user experience
  • Provide AI-powered spending insights
  • Send reminders or notifications when enabled by the user

Payment Processing

Subscription payments are processed securely by Paystack. MonieTrack does not store card numbers, bank account details, or payment credentials.

AI Features

AI-powered insights are based on user-entered information and are provided for organization and planning support only. They should not be treated as professional advice.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user information. However, no online system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

No Regulated Money Services

MonieTrack does not provide banking, lending, investment, wallet, money transfer, or regulated money management services. Users remain responsible for their own spending decisions and personal records.
